LPU WON AIU INTER UNINERSITY YOUTH FESTIVAL OVERALL TROPHY



Celebrating Success At 37th Inter University North Zone Youth Festiva

Elated to share that we have become Overall Champion in the 37th AIU North Zone Youth Festival, outshining participants from 18 universities. Our Vertos have proudly lifted a diverse range of trophies, including the Overall Music Trophy, Overall Fine Arts Trophy, Overall Literary Trophy, Overall Dance Trophy, and 1st Runner-up Trophy (Theatre).

Enchantment Unleashed: Rang Manch 2024

The auditorium brimmed with creativity and skill as students displayed their extraordinary talents during the Rang Manch 2024. With captivating acts, the students undeniably stole the spotlight, showcasing their unwavering passion and commitment to the arts.

Harmonious Homage: Ustad Alla Rakha Sangeet Sammelan 2024 At LPU

Presented by The Division of Art and Culture in partnership with Puratan Kala Punjab Gharana Tabla Vadan Sikhya Kender, this event united students and faculty in a celebration of classical music's splendor. Honoring the legacy of the iconic tabla maestro, Ustad Alla Rakha, attendees were enthralled by stellar performances from renowned artists. It was a jubilant homage to India's classical music heritage, leaving an indelible mark on all who participated.
